03 — Why we exist
The community

Thousands of people share a project.
Almost none of them share an address.

The Liberland community has grown for a decade across borders, in chats and at the occasional festival. It has no clubhouse, no permanent calendar, no front desk.

Liberland Limited is the simplest answer to that gap: a Hong Kong company that sells a membership, runs a calendar, and helps members find a cottage on the river when they want one.

What was missing
  • — A clear front door for new members.
  • — A predictable calendar of gatherings.
  • — One office to email when you need help.
  • — A way to find a residence at Ark, with someone to walk you through it.

07 — Residences
Brokerage

Cottages and houses
at Ark Village.

We don't develop and we don't own. We introduce members to homes sold by the Serbian developer of Ark Village in Apatin, on the Danube.

How the brokerage works
  1. Member requests an introduction through Liberland Limited.
  2. We confirm availability with the developer; we coordinate the visit.
  3. The sale is concluded directly between the buyer and the developer in Serbia.
  4. Liberland Limited is paid a brokerage fee by the developer on completion.

Three lines of revenue.
All paid for services rendered.

What we never do
  • — We do not operate a fund or any pooled vehicle.
  • — We do not hold client money or custody assets.
  • — We do not sell shares, tokens, or any security.
  • — We do not own the residences we introduce.
What that means in practice
  • — Card and wire payments only, into a Hong Kong corporate account.
  • — Standard small-company accounting and annual filings in Hong Kong.
  • — KYC on members at Platinum and Founder tiers.
  • — Receipted, line-item invoices on every contract.
