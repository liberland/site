About the Chamber

The Chamber of Commerce of Liberland is an independent entity, bringing together members voluntarily based on mutual business interests. It is the foundation of a future global network of bilateral chambers, connecting businesses in Liberland and partner countries.

The key objectives of the Chamber of Commerce are (see full Statute)
  • Promoting Liberlandian entrepreneurs
  • Facilitating business activities
  • Facilitating contacts between Liberlandian and foreign entrepreneurs;
  • Representing the common interests of the Chamber’s members
  • Promoting economic, social, and cultural cooperation between Liberland and other countries.
  • Promoting, supporting, and seeking opportunities for the economic development of the entire region of Slavonia

Member’s rights

  • use the services of the Chamber and participate in its operations
  • submit motions pertaining to the Chamber operations
  • participate in the General Meetings
  • active and passive electoral rights pertaining to the Board of the Chamber

Members may, but do not have to be registered under Liberland jurisdiction.

Member’s obligations

  • base their operations on principles of professional ethics and standards of fair conduct,
  • observe the provisions of the Statutes of the Chamber as well as the regulations and resolutions of the General Meetings and Board of the Chamber
  • participate in the performance of the Chamber statutory objectives
  • consider participating through a regular voluntary membership fee

Regular membership is subject to an annual voluntary fee

Main activities of the Chamber and benefits

  • establishing and developing economic cooperation with domestic and foreign entities
  • opening bilateral Chambers of Commerce with partner countries
  • collecting, analysing and distributing information supporting business operations of its members;
  • offering support for its members' business initiatives,
  • organizing and co-organizing fairs, exhibitions, conferences, meetings or webinars in Liberland and abroad
