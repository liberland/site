The History of Liberland

Free Republic of Liberland is a sovereign state located between Croatia and Serbia on the west bank of the Danube river. The nearest towns are Zmajevac (Croatia) and Bački Monoštor or Apatin (in Serbia). The country is based on moto Live and Let Live” , following the rules of free trade and free market with absolute minimal state interference into people’s lives and business activities. As the history showed, it is right this model, that brings the highest level of development to the society, demonstrated on examples such as Singapore, Hong Kong, Liechtenstein, Switzerland, Monaco and other.

With its size similar to Gibraltar or 3x larger than Monaco in combination with free trade and free society rules, it represents an enormous economical potential for the whole region. There are more than 500.000 registered users and 100.000 eligible applicants for Liberland Citizenship.

Liberland, as the first sovereign country in the human history, is coming with tokenized statehood - model, where freedoms in the society fixed in the smartcontract, to make it unchangeable, unmodifiable and guaranteed. Liberland Merit - the cryptoasset on top of that represent time holder’s voting power, currency and collateral for decentralised justice in one. This model is far ahead of what traditional states and societies are capable to even consider and represents model for the future societies arrangements.
