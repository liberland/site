The History of Liberland

The Free Republic of Liberland is a sovereign state located between Croatia and Serbia on the west bank of the Danube river. The nearest towns are Zmajevac (Croatia) and Bački Monoštor or Apatin (Serbia). The motto of the country is “To Live and Let Live”, following the rules of free trade and free market with absolute minimal State interference into people’s lives and business activities. Historically, this model has brought the highest level of development to societies, as demonstrated in countries like Singapore, Hong Kong, Liechtenstein, Switzerland, Monaco, and others.

Tokenized statehood

Liberland, as the first sovereign country in History, will be based on a tokenized statehood model, where social freedoms are fixed in unchangeable, unmodifiable, and guaranteed smart contracts. On top of that, the Liberland Merit is the all-in-one cryptoasset that represents the holder’s voting power, currency, and collateral for a decentralised justice. This model is far ahead of what traditional states and societies are able to even consider, and will serve as a model for future societies.

Future

Liberland has attracted a unique business community striving for excellence on a global level, comprising top entrepreneurs and companies from many industries, both high-tech: information technologies, cryptocurrencies, data security and cryptography, blockchain, innovation; as well as from more traditional industries: energy and renewables, hospitality, financial services, healthcare, transportation, manufacturing, and many others.
