What is Liberland?

The Free Republic of Liberland is a sovereign state located between Croatia and Serbia on the west bank of the Danube river.

Liberland came into existence due to a border dispute between Croatia and Serbia. This area along the west bank of the Danube river is not claimed by Croatia, Serbia or any other country. It was therefore terra nullius, a no man’s land, until Vít Jedlička seized the opportunity and on 13 April 2015 formed a new state in this territory – Liberland. Its total area of approximately 7 km² is now the third smallest sovereign state, after the Vatican and Monaco.
